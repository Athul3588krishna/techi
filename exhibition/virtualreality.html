Virtual Reality

Introduction to VR

  • What is VR? Virtual Reality is a simulated experience that can be similar to or completely different from the real world.
  • Applications: VR is used in gaming, education, healthcare, architecture, and entertainment.

Popular VR Technologies:

  1. Oculus Quest:
    • A standalone VR headset offering an immersive experience.
  2. HTC Vive:
    • Known for its high-quality VR hardware and room-scale experiences.
  3. PlayStation VR:
    • Brings VR gaming to console users.
  4. Google Cardboard:
    • An affordable entry point to VR using smartphones.

Why Explore VR?

  • Creates immersive learning and training experiences.
  • Enhances entertainment with interactive environments.
  • Revolutionizes industries like real estate and healthcare.
