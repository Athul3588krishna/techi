IoT Projects

Introduction to IoT

  • What is IoT? IoT connects physical devices to the internet, enabling smart interactions and automation.
  • Applications: IoT is used in agriculture, healthcare, smart homes, and industrial automation.

Project Highlights:

  1. Pet Plant Monitor:
    • Helps analyze plant climate conditions via smart devices.
    • Monitors soil moisture, air quality, temperature, and humidity.
    • Accessible from any smart device through the internet.
  2. Mood Lamp:
    • Offers customizable and soothing color modes with gradients and presets.
    • Controlled via a website for seamless adjustments.
    • Uses an ambient light sensor to adjust brightness automatically.
