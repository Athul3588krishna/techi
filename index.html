About The Event

Trinity TechFest is the third edition of our annual technical festival, following the success of Spectra and Spectra 2.0. This year, we bring a new name and identity to our celebration of technology and innovation. The name Trinity symbolizes the unity of ideas, innovation, and knowledge, reflecting the collaborative spirit of our college. It represents a harmonious blend of past achievements, present endeavors, and future aspirations. Join us as we explore cutting-edge advancements, showcase creativity, and celebrate the ever-evolving world of technology!

About the College

K.R. Sree Narayana College, Valanchery, is a prominent educational institution located in Valanchery, Kerala. Affiliated with the University of Calicut, the college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in arts, science, and commerce. Established with a mission to provide quality education, it upholds the ideals of Sree Narayana Guru, emphasizing equality, social progress, and knowledge for all. The college is known for its vibrant academic environment, dedicated faculty, and active participation in extracurricular and community development activities. It aims to nurture holistic development and empower students for a successful future.
